Delish Match : Match3 & Design is a fun culinary simulator that lets players become the head chef of their own restaurant. Manage the kitchen, create amazing dishes, serve customers, and grow your restaurant. Every dish presents a new challenge, and satisfied customers will return again and again. As you progress, unlock new ingredients, recipes, and equipment to make your restaurant the best in town. Delish Match : Match3 & Design offers many levels with unique challenges and culinary adventures!
